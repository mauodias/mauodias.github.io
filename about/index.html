Who am I?

Graduated from Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio Grande do Sul in 2017 with a degree in Computer Science, I work in the IT industry since 2010. After a brief adventure in the technical support realm, I became a software developer and never left this world (and don’t plan to).

During this journey, I had colleagues in the following companies:

  • Valid
  • SAP
  • Dell
  • Lakes Environmental
  • Sindus ANDRITZ
  • Hogarth Worldwide

And today I’m a Consultant Developer at ThoughtWorks, working with IT infrastructure and devops.

Still on tech

Technologist by craft, teacher by passion.

One of my biggest, if not the biggest incentive to continue in a IT career is to propagate the knowledge that I acquired and continue acquiring over the years. Through several initiatives, I’ve been trying to spread the technology knowledge to more people, especially those who wouldn’t have conditions to do that, thanks to the endless inequities of our society.

And after 6PM?

No one lives only to work (thankfully). Among my non technological passions are cycling, photography and cooking. If any details about it will ever be public, no one knows. (SPOILER: Instagram).
